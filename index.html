Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about FlowStack.

How is FlowStack different from GitHub/GitLab? +

FlowStack complements your existing Git hosting by providing enhanced CI/CD, deployment automation, and team collaboration features in one integrated platform.

Does FlowStack support on-premise deployment? +

Yes! Our Enterprise plan supports on-premise and VPC deployments with full administrative control.

Can I use my existing Git provider? +

Absolutely. FlowStack integrates seamlessly with GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, and other popular Git providers.

How does the free plan work? +

Our free plan includes all core features with up to 3 users, unlimited public repositories, and basic CI/CD minutes. No credit card required to start.

What languages and frameworks do you support? +

FlowStack is language-agnostic and supports all major frameworks including React, Vue, Angular, Node.js, Python, Ruby, Java, Go, and more.
